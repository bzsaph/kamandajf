5 Things to Know Before Your Kigali Airport Transfer

2026  ·  4 min read

Arriving in Kigali should be the easy part of your trip. A well-planned airport transfer takes the stress out of landing in a new city — here is how to make yours seamless.

1. Book ahead and share your flight number

When you book in advance and share your flight details, our team tracks your arrival in real time. If your flight is early or delayed, your chauffeur adjusts automatically — no waiting, no extra charges.

2. Look for the meet-and-greet

Your driver waits in the arrivals area with a name sign, ready to help with luggage and guide you straight to a clean, comfortable vehicle.

3. Choose the right vehicle

Travelling solo, as a family, or with a delegation? From executive sedans to luxury vans and coaches, pick the vehicle that fits your group and luggage.

4. Have your destination ready

Hotel, office or residence — share the exact address so we plan the fastest, safest route across the city.

5. Pay your way

Confirm your quote upfront with transparent pricing. No surprises, just a smooth ride from the runway to your door.

Top 7 Destinations to Explore in Rwanda This Season

2026  ·  6 min read

The land of a thousand hills is packed with unforgettable experiences. Here are seven destinations our tour team loves — and can take you to in comfort.

1. Volcanoes National Park

Home to the famous mountain gorillas and golden monkeys, set against the dramatic Virunga volcanoes.

2. Lake Kivu

Relax on one of Africa's great lakes — beaches, boat rides and stunning sunsets in Rubavu and Karongi.

3. Nyungwe Forest

Walk the canopy bridge and trek through ancient rainforest teeming with chimpanzees and birdlife.

4. Akagera National Park

Rwanda's Big Five savannah safari — lions, elephants, rhinos and more on the eastern plains.

5. Kigali City Tour

Clean, green and vibrant — explore markets, museums, the Genocide Memorial and a thriving food scene.

6. Musanze Caves

Explore dramatic lava-tube caves on the way to the volcanoes region.

7. Kigali to Lake Kivu Scenic Drive

One of Africa's most beautiful road trips — winding through endless green hills and tea plantations.
